### 🏛️ Financial and Risk Analyst — JPMorgan Chase & Co., USA
**November 2024 – Present**

- Analyze 10,000+ weekly card, ACH, debit, and wire transactions using enterprise fraud detection platforms to identify financial anomalies, risk patterns, and emerging fraud typologies, contributing to financial reporting delivered to compliance and operations leadership.
- Build and maintain daily and weekly financial reports in SQL and Excel tracking alert volumes, fraud losses, dispute outcomes, and trend metrics, supporting month-end close reporting cycles and FP&A review processes.
- Conduct variance and root-cause analysis on chargeback and dispute data, aligning evidence packages to Visa, Mastercard, and Reg E timelines to improve dispute win rates and reduce unrecovered losses.
- Develop tiered risk-scoring frameworks and recommend rule and threshold enhancements to fraud strategy teams, contributing to measurable reductions in false-positive alert volumes and analyst review time.
- Perform cost analysis on fraud loss trends to support operational budget reviews and efficiency recommendations presented to senior leadership.
- Validate customer identity, account ownership, and behavioral signals using KYC data, transaction history, and IP and device intelligence to support P&L-impacting financial investigations.
- Partner with AML and Compliance teams to ensure financial investigations align with BSA/AML, OFAC, and internal policy controls, supporting documentation for SAR-relevant cases in accordance with GAAP and regulatory standards.
- Maintain SLA compliance and audit-grade case quality while managing a high-volume review queue.
